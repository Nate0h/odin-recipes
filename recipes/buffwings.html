Buffalo Wings

Crispy and spicy homemade Buffalo Wings!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Sugar
  • Frank's Red Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Prep Wings: Separate drums and flats, preheat oven.
  2. Make Dry Season: Combine baking powder, salt, and garlic powder.
  3. Bake: Center of the oven for 50 min, flipping halfway.
  4. Make Buffalo Sauce: In a medium bowl, stir together melted butter, sugar, and hot sauce.
  5. Toss Wings: Coat and toss wings in buffalo sauce.
