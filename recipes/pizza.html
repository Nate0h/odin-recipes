Cheese Pizza Recipe

Everyone's favorite classic cheese pizza!

Ingredients

  • Homemade Pizza Crust
  • 3/4 cup of Pizza Sauce
  • 8oz of sliced Mozzarella cheese
  • 2-3 Tbs of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Ground Basil

Steps

  1. Prep Oven: Preheat oven to 475F
  2. Top the Pizza: Spread sauce on pizza, then the sliced mozzarella and parmesan cheese. Sprinkle basil
  3. Bake: Bake pizza for 12-15 min
  4. Serve: Slice Pizza and Serrve
