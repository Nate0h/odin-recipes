Taco Recipe

Simple and easy to make ground beef tacos, that everyone loves!

Ingredients

  • 1-pound lean ground beef
  • 1 Jar El Paso Chunky Salsa
  • Taco Shells
  • 1/2 head of shredded lettuce
  • 3/4 cups of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of shredded Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Prep Ground Beef: Cook beef in 10-inch skillet over medium heat 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until brown; drain.
  2. Mix salsa with Beef: Stir salsa into beef. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly; reduce heat to medium-low. Cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Pour beef mixture into large serving bowl.
  3. Serve Tacos: Heat taco shells as directed on package. Serve taco shells with beef mixture, lettuce, tomato and cheese.
